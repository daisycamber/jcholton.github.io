Flashcards for Words and Articles

Jasper Holton | September 11, 2015

These are some flashcards I created for the words and articles we have learned so far. For best results, look at the word and then say it twice aloud, once in single form and once in plural form with the article. Enjoy!

Instructions: Move back and forth through the set using the arrow keys or onscreen buttons. Click the card to flip it and see the answer. The program will read aloud the answers to you. Press the star button in the upper right corner to mark a term you need to review. Press the "Options" button to edit how the cards are displayed.
